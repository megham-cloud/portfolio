My Skills

User Research

I utilize user research in order to understand the users, what makes them tick, and what they enjoy. Ultimately, after conducting user interviews and surveys and analyzing the results through means such as affinity diagrams, I create a user persona to really empathize with the users and start to define our problem.

User Interface Design

I use my knowledge of color theory and other laws like Gestalt's principles to determine a design that not only looks visually pleasing, but has real intent and meaning behind the visual direction taken. I develop and use a style guide or enhance a preexisting one to determine the look of every design project.

Web Development

Through my knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Jquery, and Bootstrap, I can not only work on the front-end development of websites (like this very website), but I can also understand and communicate with developers who I need to work with when I am taking a UX/UI designer role.
